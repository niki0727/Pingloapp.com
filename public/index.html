Safety-first by default

Pinglo combines moderation, privacy controls, and retention rules to protect users.

  • Public feed has no public photos. Proof photos are private and verification-only.
  • Report + block tools are available directly in chat flows.
  • Proof photos auto-delete 30 days after return confirmation.
  • Meet in public places for handoff.
  • Never share bank details or personal IDs.
  • Use in-app confirmation to close every return.
